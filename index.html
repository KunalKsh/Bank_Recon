Reconciliation Engine

A cross-referencing system that utilizes a hierarchical 7-pass protocol to reconcile data across disparate bank and manual ledgers.

The Priority Protocol

Logic Tier Definition
P0

Direct Alignment

Matches where both Date and Amount are identical across sources.

P1

Decimal Offset

Exact date matches with a minor amount variance of exactly ±1.

P2

Fee Deduction

Exact date matches with specific variance for fees: ±5, 10, 15, or 20.

P3

Bank Latency

Matches where bank posting occurs exactly +1 day later with exact amounts.

P4

Date/Round

Window of -2 to +5 days with exact rounded amount matching.

P5

Tight Tolerance

Window of -2 to +4 days with a maximum variance of ±25.

P6

Last Recovery

Broad window of -2 to +7 days with a variance up to ±100.
